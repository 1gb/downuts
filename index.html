About

Easily digestible investment information delivered fresh daily.

DOWNUTS are a subscripation-based delivery service for the hungry investor. Simply sign up through out website and delicious donuts containing the latest stock information will be delivered to your door. Need to impress the boss? Have an important investor meeting coming up? DOWNUTS are the perfect way to demonstrate your knowledge of current financial trends in a hip, unique way. Why wait? Order DOWNUTS today!

How it works

It couldn't be simpler.

Step 1.

Choose your plan and stocks. We provide weekly or daily (M-F) delivery options.

Step 2.

Our skilled bakers prepare fresh doughnuts and hand-pipe the latest stock information onto them via icing.

Step 3.

Your DOWNUTS are delivered to your home or office.
